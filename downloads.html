Cartography Downloads

The Cartography Department recommends the use of GIMP to edit XCF files and Inkscape to edit SVG files.
PLEASE NOTE: Your browser may warn you when downloading files from this website. This is due to the low volume of downloads and should not be cause for concern.

Political (XCF - PNG)
Urth RP's main map. Showcases nations, cities, and subdivisions.

Supplemental (XCF - PNG)
Includes one of the styles of wikimap and a blank map.

Wiki Locator (XCF - PNG)
Used for locator maps on TEPwiki, which are usually in a nation's infobox. The highlighted nation should use #c22335.

Topography (PNG)
Topographic map of Urth. Only available as a PNG.

Climate (XCF - PNG)
Urth's climate map. Uses the Köppen climate classification.

Time Zones (XCF - PNG)
Time zones of Urth. Please contact Jesse (Tuva) to be added to this map.

Currents (SVG - PNG)
Map of Urth's winds and major water currents. Used to build the climate map.
